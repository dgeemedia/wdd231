About 9jaVault

9jaVault is Nigeria’s premier investment rate comparison platform, built to solve the challenge of scattered investment data across banks. We offer tools that allow users to compare interest rates on fixed deposits, treasury bills, and other financial products from top-tier institutions.

Our mission is to promote financial transparency, empower informed investing, and increase the savings culture across Nigeria. We believe everyone—regardless of background—should have access to data that helps grow their money.
